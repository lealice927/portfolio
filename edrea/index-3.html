About Me

Hi! My name is Alice Le. My interest in web development and all things tech began at an adolescent age originating from building computers with my father. It lead to my interests in problem solving, creating and building things, being meticulous, creative, and patient. In high school I decided I wanted to further expand my knowledge on how websites were built, so I signed up to take a web design class. There I had my first experience utilizing HTML and CSS.

Coming from a family background of entrepreneurs, I decided to pursue Business Management and Supply Chain Management for my undergrad. Having years of experience working in management and customer service in other tech industries, my passion and curiosity for software development continued to blossom.
